Holiday hours

We close early (4pm) on public holiday eves and remain closed on major public holidays. Updated hours are always posted here and on our Instagram.

Walk-ins

Walk-ins are welcome for cuts and blow-drys, subject to availability. Colour and treatment services require a booked appointment.

Cancellations

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule, so we can offer the slot to another guest.
